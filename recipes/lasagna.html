Lasagna

This classic Lasagna Recipe is cheesy, saucy and full of flavor!
Make this easy lasagna recipe for a family dinner or to feed a crowd.

    Ingridients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt, Pepper and Italian Seasoning
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Egg
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

    How to make lasagna

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Brown the ground beef with onion and garlic.
  3. To the pan, add the marinara sauce,
    Italian seasoning and fresh parsley.
    Simmer the sauce for 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, stir together the ricotta cheese and an egg.
    Shred the mozzarella cheese.
  5. Layer the lasagna.

    How to layer Lasagna

  1. Spread a layer of sauce in the bottom of your baking dish.
  2. Make a layer of 3 noodles over the sauce.
  3. Spread 1/3 of the remaining sauce over the noodles. Spoon on half of the ricotta and egg mixture.
    Sprinkle on 1/3 of the mozzarella and 1/3 of the Parmesan cheese.
  4. Repeat the noodle, sauce and cheese layer one more time.
  5. For the top layer, use the remaining 3 noodles and spread on the rest of the sauce. Sprinkle on the
    rest of the mozzarella and Parmesan.
